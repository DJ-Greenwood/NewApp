Tips for Creating Memorable Characters

  • Be specific and detailed

    The more details you provide about your character's personality, quirks, and background, the more authentic they'll feel.

  • Give them flaws and strengths

    Well-rounded characters have both positive traits and weaknesses that make them more relatable and interesting.

  • Consider their unique voice

    How a character speaks—their vocabulary, catchphrases, or speech patterns—can bring them to life in conversations.
